> *A hands-on route from amateur to command in real estate development, one end-to-end build at a time. A route to command, not a degree.*

This repo is my run at real command of real estate development, built the way I actually learn: by making the field work end to end, one system at a time, with AI as the multiplier that makes it realistic on my own hours.

## How I'm learning this

I pick a real capability in real estate development and build it end to end, then prove it with a concrete self-check and a plain-language teach-back, instead of collecting chapters I skim and forget. AI is what makes the pace possible: it lets me build, wire up current tools, and run real scenarios in hours instead of semesters. Expect this to fill up over time, one system at a time, each one runnable and checked against something concrete. What I get from it is real, working command of the field and a public body of work that shows it, not a certificate standing in for the reps.

## What this track covers

Algorithmic underwriting and pro-forma modeling, an automated valuation model, GIS site selection, and a data-driven developer product. Each build applies the field end to end, ships a runnable artifact with a concrete self-check, and proves the skill with a teach-back.

**What it isn't.** A degree or credential, and no claim past what each build's self-check proves. Raising capital is a securities activity, so every memo, pitch, or syndication build here is an educational mock-up and not a solicitation. Any real use needs securities counsel. Not legal or financial advice.
